Ao Phrao Bay

The Experience

Koh Kood is among the most pristine islands of Thailand. Situated in Ao Phrao Bay, our perfect beachfront location offers direct access to a beautiful 950-metre stretch of white sand and crystal-clear water.

Based on our family's intimate knowledge of the area, you are invited to join our exclusive and unique "Unseen Sunshine" experience. Discover our natural setting nestled among coconut groves and untouched jungle beyond.

Our Story

Built in 2009, The Sunshine Resort is a sanctuary run by a family who has lived here for generations. We pride ourselves on a warm welcome and tasty local food, with freshly caught seafood among the specialities. It's an authentic taste of island life, passed down through our heritage.
